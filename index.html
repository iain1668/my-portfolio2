About Me

Hello, I am Iain Winter. I have a passion for helping people, whether it’s improving their health and fitness or guiding them to achieve their best selves. Originally from Scotland, I moved to Australia at the age of 24 to seek new opportunities and to explore the world we live in.

With over 15 years of experience in the sports nutrition industry, I reached a point where personal development was no longer achievable and sought further growth by joining tier 1 companies. My journey took me to Canon, where I worked with some of the best professionals in the industry, and then to Hilti, where I faced new challenges.

Outside of work, I have dedicated over 23 years to my own health and fitness and in more recent year, Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (no-gi), which has instilled in me a deep sense of discipline and dedication. My professional ethos is driven by a commitment to continuous improvement and making a meaningful impact.

Throughout my career, I have mentored hundreds of staff, developed advanced reporting tools, and been recognized as a pioneer in the sports nutrition industry in Western Australia. My assertive yet understanding approach has helped many colleagues grow and excel.

I am now looking to transition into a role where I can help people more directly, leveraging my experience and passion for making a positive difference in their lives.
